CQC Inspection Readiness

Step 1: press Generate. Step 2: to print the copy for the inspector, just press Print inspector copy — it does the rest (switches to the inspector version and takes you to the confirmation step). You do not need to change the mode yourself.

This is a sign-off step, not a barrier. Anyone may print the pack — just make sure a clinician or practice manager has reviewed the figures and is happy for it to be ticked. If that's been done, go ahead.
