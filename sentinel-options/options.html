Alpha feature — review before relying on these alerts
Library alerts are starter templates derived from published prescribing-safety guidelines (PINCER, NICE, MHRA). They have not been clinically validated in this codebase and individual rules may match incorrectly, miss cases, or fire on irrelevant medications. You are responsible for reviewing match terms, thresholds, and behaviour against your local formulary and patient population before clinical use. By acknowledging, you accept that these alerts are decision support only — they do not replace clinical judgement, and the suite author accepts no liability for their use.
